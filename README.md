# digital-literacy-project


## Introduction
This project is about understanding digital literacy and how we use technology in our daily life. Through different tasks, I learned about online safety, communication, and using digital platforms effectively.

---

## Task 1: Infographic
In this task, I created an infographic using Canva based on digital literacy. I included topics like safe internet practices and email etiquette. It helped me learn how to present information in a simple and visual way.

---

## Task 2: Digital Portfolio
For this task, I created and updated my profiles on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. This helped me understand the importance of having an online presence and how these platforms are useful for students.

---

## Task 3: Coding and Google Form
I solved a basic coding problem on HackerRank and also created a Google Form quiz related to digital literacy. This task improved my basic coding understanding and helped me learn how to create online forms.

---

## Task 4: Email and Social Media Etiquette
In this task, I wrote two professional emails and listed some do’s and don’ts of social media. It helped me understand how to communicate properly in a formal way.

---

## Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness
I selected phishing as a cybercrime topic and wrote a short case study along with prevention tips. I also learned how to report cybercrime using official platforms.

---

## Conclusion
Overall, this project helped me improve my knowledge about digital tools, online safety, and communication. It was a good learning experience and improved my digital skills.
